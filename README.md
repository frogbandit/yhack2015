#![alt text](http://i68.tinypic.com/9iqnv5.png "Logo Title Text 1")

**Centr is the simple way to meet up with friends.** Just enter you and your friends' locations, and what kind of place you want to meet up at (cafe, restaurant, or park), and see which places are most convenient for you. 

![alt text](http://i64.tinypic.com/a334va.png "App Screenshot 1")

Made with love at [YHack 2015](http://www.yhack.org/live/) by **Josh Hellerstein, James Xue, Jennifer McCleary, and Riancy Li**, with idea credit to Holly Zhou. Tools used: Python, Flask, Google Maps Javascript API, Materialize

Live version is available at **[gocentr.me](http://gocentr.me)**.
